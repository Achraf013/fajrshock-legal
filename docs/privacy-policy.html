Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

FajrShock ("we", "our", or "the app") is an Islamic prayer alarm and tracking application developed by Achraf Omar Ouali, based in Tilburg, Netherlands. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

FajrShock requests access to your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area and to determine the Qibla direction. Your location data is processed entirely on your device using the adhan prayer time calculation library. We do not transmit, store, or share your location data with any server or third party.

Device Sensor Data

Certain alarm missions use your device's sensors, including the accelerometer (for shake and squat detection), pedometer (for step counting), and magnetometer (for Qibla compass). All sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted or stored beyond the active session.

Subscription and Purchase Data

If you subscribe to FajrShock Premium, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, to verify your subscription status and manage entitlements. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier associated with your device. We do not have access to your name, email address, payment method, or billing information through this process. For more details, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Locally Stored Data

FajrShock stores your preferences, alarm configurations, prayer tracking history, streak data, and Ramadan progress locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to any server. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

What we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal contact information
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not collect or store passwords
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use cookies
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information processed by FajrShock is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Calculating prayer times based on your geographic location
  • Determining the Qibla direction from your current position
  • Scheduling alarm notifications at the correct prayer times
  • Tracking your prayer completion, streaks, and Ramadan progress
  • Verifying your subscription status for premium features
  • Operating alarm missions that require sensor input

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal information. Your data is protected by the security measures built into your device's operating system, including encryption when your device is locked.

The only external communication made by FajrShock is with RevenueCat's servers, solely for the purpose of verifying subscription status. This communication uses encrypted HTTPS connections and involves only an anonymous device identifier.

4. Third-Party Services

FajrShock uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - Subscription management and purchase verification. RevenueCat processes an anonymous device identifier to manage your subscription status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store - Payment processing for subscriptions. All payment transactions are handled by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Expo / React Native - Application framework. No user data is transmitted to Expo. Expo Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

FajrShock is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors. If you believe a child under 13 has somehow provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the concern.

6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access - You can view all your data directly within the app, as it is stored locally on your device.
  • Right to Deletion - You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since we do not store data on external servers, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.
  • Right to Portability - Your data is stored locally and is accessible through your device.
  • Right to Object - Since we do not process data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making, there is no processing to object to.
  • Right to Restrict Processing - You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function but will require a manually set location for prayer time calculations.

7. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed. We do not retain any data on external servers. When you uninstall FajrShock, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. RevenueCat may retain anonymous subscription records according to their own retention policy.

8. International Data Transfers

FajrShock does not transfer your personal data internationally. All data processing occurs on your device. The only external communication is with RevenueCat servers for subscription verification, which involves only anonymous identifiers. RevenueCat is based in the United States and complies with applicable data protection regulations.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of FajrShock after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
